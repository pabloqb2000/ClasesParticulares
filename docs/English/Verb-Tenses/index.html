Present Simple

infinitive (he/she/it) + -s
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I work. ✅ I go. ✅ He works. ✅ He goes.
❌ I don't work. ❌ I don't go. ❌ He doesn't work. ❌ He doesn't go.
❔ Do I work? ❔ Do I go? ❔ Does he work? ❔ Does he go?
  • Algo que ocurre de forma repetida (Ej: Trabajo en un hospital)
  • Algo que nunca ocurre (Ej: Yo no como pescado)
  • Hechos que son ciertos en general (Ej: El cielo es azul, La lluvia cae de las nubes)
  • Horarios, listas de tareas,...
  • Con qué frecuencia ocurre algo (Ej: Hago deporte todos los días)
  • Una acción que ocurre después de otra (en una lista de instrucciones, por ejemplo en una receta)
  • Con verbos como gustar, odiar, creer, tener,...
  • [Zero conditional]
Palabras indicadoras:
Every day, sometimes, always, often, usually, seldom, never, first ... then

Present Continuous

to be (am/are/is) + infinitive + -ing
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I'm working. ✅ I'm going. ✅ He's working. ✅ He's going.
❌ I'm not working. ❌ I'm not going. ❌ He isn't working. ❌ He isn't going.
❔ Am I working? ❔ Am I going? ❔ Is he working? ❔ Is he going?
  • Algo que está ocurriendo en ese mismo momento.
  • Un plan o una cita que tienes para un futuro cercano.
Palabras indicadoras:
Now, at the moment, look!, listen!

Past Simple

regular: infinitive + -ed
irregular: 2nd column of table of irregular verbs
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I worked. ✅ I went. ✅ He worked. ✅ He went.
❌ I didn't work. ❌ I didn't go. ❌ He didn't work. ❌ He didn't go.
❔ Did I work? ❔ Did I go? ❔ Did he work? ❔ Did he go?
  • Algo que ocurrió en el pasado, normalmente indicando cuándo ocurrió (sin ninguna conexión explícita con el presente)
Palabras indicadoras:
Last week/month/..., 2 days/weeks/... ago, in 1990, yesterday

Past Continuous

was/were + infinitive + -ing
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I was working. ✅ I was going. ✅ He was working. ✅ He was going.
❌ I wasn't working. ❌ I wasn't going. ❌ He wasn't working. ❌ He wasn't going.
❔ Was I working? ❔ Was I going? ❔ Was he working? ❔ Was he going?
  • Algo que ocurrió entre medias de de otra acción
  • Algo que estaba ocurriendo en el pasado y no sabes si sigue ocurriendo o no
Palabras indicadoras:
While, when (cuando aparece when y no es la primera palabra de una pregunta)

Present Perfect

have/has + past participle*
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I have worked. ✅ I have gone. ✅ He has worked. ✅ He has gone.
❌ I haven't worked. ❌ I haven't gone. ❌ He hasn't worked. ❌ He hasn't gone.
❔ Have I worked? ❔ Have I gone? ❔ Has he worked? ❔ Has he gone?
  • Algo que comenzó en el pasado y sigue ocurriendo en el presente
  • Algo que nunca ha pasado o que no ha pasado todavía
  • Algo que ocurrió en el pasado y tiene una conexión explícita con el presente
Palabras indicadoras:
Just, yet, never, ever, already, so far, up to now, since, for 5 days/weeks/months, recently

Present Perfect Continuous

have/has + been + infinitive + -ing
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I have been working. ✅ I have been going. ✅ He has been working. ✅ He has been going.
❌ I haven't been working. ❌ I haven't been going. ❌ He hasn't been working. ❌ He hasn't been going.
❔ Have I been working? ❔ Have I been going? ❔ Has he been working? ❔ Has he been going?
  • Acción que empezó en el pasado y justo acaba de terminar.
  • Indicar o preguntar durante cuanto tiempo ha estado ocurriendo algo.
  • Enfasis: duración de la acción
Palabras indicadoras:
All day, the whole day, how long, since, for 5 days/weeks/months

Past Perfect (Simple)

had + past participle*
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I had worked. ✅ I had gone. ✅ He had worked. ✅ He had gone.
❌ I hadn't worked. ❌ I hadn't gone. ❌ He hadn't worked. ❌ He hadn't gone.
❔ Had I worked? ❔ Had I gone? ❔ Had he worked? ❔ Had he gone?
  • Una acción que terminó antes de que empezara otra.
  • Algo que no había ocurrido nunca o no había ocurrido todavía en un momento pasado.
Palabras indicadoras:
Already, just, never

Past Perfect Continuous

had + been + infinitive + ing
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I had been working. ✅ I had been going. ✅ He had been working. ✅ He had been going.
❌ I hadn't been working. ❌ I hadn't been going. ❌ He hadn't been working. ❌ He hadn't been going.
❔ Had I been working? ❔ Had I been going? ❔ Had he been working? ❔ Had he been going?
  • Para decir/preguntar cuánto tiempo había estado ocurriendo algo antes de un cierto momento en el pasado
Palabras indicadoras:
How long, since, for

Future Simple (will)

will + infinitive
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I'll work. ✅ I'll go. ✅ He'll work. ✅ He'll go.
❌ I won't work. ❌ I won't go. ❌ He won't work. ❌ He won't go.
❔ Will I work? ❔ Will I go? ❔ Will he work? ❔ Will he go?
  • Predicciones sobre el futuro (hechos o cosas de las que estamos muy seguros).
  • Algo que se te ha ocurrido hacer mientras hablas, sin haberlo decidido antes.
  • Para hacer promesas, peticiones, ofertas,...
  • [First conditional]

Future Simple (going to)

to be (am/are/is) + going to + infinitive
1ª persona 3ª persona
✅ I'm going to work. ✅ I'm going to go. ✅ He's going to work. ✅ He's going to go.
❌ I'm not going to work. ❌ I'm not going to go. ❌ He's not going to work. ❌ He's not going to go.
❔ Am I going to work? ❔ Am I going to go? ❔ Is he going to work? ❔ Is he going to go?
  • Algo que vas a hacer en el futuro y que ya habías decidido antes.
  • Una predicción basada en algo que acabas de ver/oir/saber justo ahora.
  • Una predicción basada en algo que crees/opinas.
